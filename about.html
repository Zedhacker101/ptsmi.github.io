What is PTSMI?

PTSMI - City Of Perfection

PTSMI aka City of Perfection (COP) is an end-time movement of the latter rain annointing; founded by Christ on His deep teachings, out to perfect the saints by progressively building them up [ especially character wise ] into the full image of Christ, unite the body of Christ (or break denominational barriers), preparing the earthly church for the glorious rapture and even an eternity in God and to bring the influence of God over the seven mountains of influence; RELIGION [Spirituality], GOVERNMENT [Politics], CELEBRATION [Art, Entertainment and Sports], ECONOMY [Science/Technology & Business] FAMILY, EDUCATION, MEDIA [Comunication].

PTSMI

Since 2015

Though PTSMI has been existing spiritually, it started on the 10th of Hoshea [October] 2015 under the earthly shepherd-ship of the PONTIFF, Chief Labourer [CL], Chief Military Officer [CMO] and Founder of PTSMI Apostle Nyenti Eta Boris.

What you should know

Mission

Making disciples of all nations, perfecting them into the whole measure of the fullness of Christ, empowering them for the fulfilment of purpose, through the teaching of God's word, and manifestation of His life and demonstration of His power

Vision

To present the church as a worthy bride of the lamb [Jesus Christ our Lord]; perfected in Holiness, Love, Faith, Liberty, Unity and Dominion.

Core Values

Worship, fellowship, Transformation/Growth,
Service, Evangelism/Discipleship, Sacrifice, Modesty,
Prayers and Love/Honor.
